#sonoravirus: 01 - SOLI in quarantena

12 March 2020 - Live Streaming - Bologna
Ascolta "SonoraVirus #1 - Giovanni Onorato" su Spreaker.

In the end of February 2020, as a collective we decided to start streaming concerts because of our new collaboration with USMA Radio.

I decided to perform the work I did for my Bachelor final project that was postponed due to the pandemic.
The performance consists on a improvisation with assisted-composition: a code written in SuperCollider is stochastically generating and performing a structure while I perform improvising with an electric bass as a sound source, elaborated via Ableton and Max For Live.
