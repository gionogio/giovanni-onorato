Elektroakustik Musik #1

5 December 2019 - Lillasalen, KMH - Stockholm

This concert was a final performance of the pieces composed during the Elektroakustisk musik course held by Marcus Wrangö.

I performed the piece I composed ad-hoc for this, named microDocks.
I had the opportunity to compose using both the mini-dome (14 speakers plus 2 subwoofers) in the studio 1D114 and Klangkupol (29 speakers plus 4 subwoofer) at Lillasalen, KMH.

The piece was composed in Ambisonics 7th Order, I used the IEM Plugins which are a useful and straightforward tool to compose with space.
