ROUND

October 2 2020 - Elettronica Collettiva Bologna

ROUND (2020) is a record of Minus - Collettivo d'improvvisazione published by Tempo Reale Collection.  ROUND is a set of rules for improvisation.

I take part of ROUND#7 (track #4) with a laptop-based set and an electric bass (with a Sovtek Black Bif Muff) as a sound source.
