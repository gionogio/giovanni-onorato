Sintagmi

October 6 2019 - Minus - Collettivo d'improvvisazione, Daniele Carcassi & Giovanni Onorato

Sintagmi, published by Switch Music Records in 2020, it is a collection of recorded improvisations.

It was an idea of the two of us (me and Daniele Carcassi) and Minus - Collettivo d'improvvisazione.
We decided to publish together mainly because there are strong connections between ours approaches to this practice.

Tracks #1, #4 and #5 are open compositions of our former teacher Francesco Giomi. While Lembi (track #2) is a free improvisation between me and Carcassi; Round#1 is the first recording of this set of rules for improvisation which Minus has later on published.
