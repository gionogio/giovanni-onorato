Festival 2 visages des musiques électroacoustiques

March 27 2019 - L’Espace Senghor - Bruxelles

The concert consisted on two moments: a performance by Francesco Giomi playing his composition Emotional Soundscapes, and then together we played his composition Back To Silence - which with the whole collettivo minus and us will be record another perfomarce of the same composition for our album Sintagmi.
