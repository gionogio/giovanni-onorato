GBM50

29 October 2021 - Conservatorio GB Martini - Bologna

GBM50 was the concert for the 50th anniversary of the introduction of Electronic Music at Conservatorio G.B. Martini in Bologna, thanks to Gian Felice Fugazza.

The concert hosted many works from teachers and notable alumni of the institution.
It was an honour to be invited to perform my recent composition No Future.
