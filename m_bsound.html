BolognaSound vol. 2 - GBM50 Edition

October 29 2021 - Various Artists

tra (2019)

The piece deals with the investigation of similarities, contrasts and – above all – ambiguities created by the juxtaposition of two different sound sources coming from synthetic and real-world domains. This idea came while I was recording a soundscape in Stockholm, in the central area of Skeppsholmen, in which a sound captured my attention.

There was a boat held by a rope to the dock and a specific sound captured my attention. Whenever the waves moved the boat, a metallic ring resonated with the peg it was attached to. The sound produced a very high-pitched, quasi-sinusoidal sound.

What sparked my interest was the idea of that sound in and out of that specific context: abstracting the sound from the dock soundscape, it could really sound as a glitch.
