Martini Elettrico~ 2019

April 11 & 12 2019 - Conservatorio G.B. Martini - Bologna

Martini Elettrico has been our electronic music festival.
This edition meant a lot for us since it was the first edition in which we were participating as a whole indipendent identity as a collective.

I played the last two days of the festival:
The 11th of April for the Live Electronics Classics in which I played the electronics of Japanese Garden by Doina Rotaru for flute (Giulia Biffi) and tape.
On the last day I joined the Banda Elettroacustica del Conservatorio for an improvisation curated by Minus - Collettivo d'Improvvisazione
