I am an electroacoustic composer and musician currently based in Stockholm.

My works are mostly related to Acousmatic Music within the domain of fixed-media works, and Electroacoustic Improvisation regarding live performances.

I obtained a Bachelor Degree in Electronic Music at Conservatorio di Bologna and now I am currently enrolled at the Electroacoustic Composition Master Program at the Royal College of Music in Stockholm.

My thesis was about the development of a SuperCollider code able to generate and perform a composition with whom I could improvise with an electric bass used as a sound source, processed with Ableton Live and M4L.
