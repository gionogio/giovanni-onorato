I am an electroacoustic composer and musician currently based in Stockholm.

My works are mostly related to acousmatic music within the domain of fixed-media works, and electroacoustic improvisation regarding live performances.

I obtained a bachelor's degree in Electronic Music at Conservatorio di Bologna and now I am enrolled at the Electroacoustic Composition master program at the Royal College of Music in Stockholm.

My thesis was about the development of a SuperCollider code able to generate and perform a stochastic composition with whom I could improvise with an electric bass used as a sound source, processed with Ableton Live and M4L.

In the last years I had the wonderful opportunity to take part in great music events like: Angelica - Festival Internazionale di Musica (Bologna), Bologna Modern (Bologna), Festival 2 visages des musiques électroacoustiques (Brussels), Ljudoljud (Stockholm), Martini Elettrico (Bologna), TRK Sound Club - Tempo Reale (Firenze).

I am a founding member of Elettronica Collettiva Bologna, a collective based in Bologna for promoting electroacoustic music, and Senza Distinzione di Genere lab, a electroacoustic-free improvisation continous workshop taking place at Labàs (Bologna).
